Sex & Nudity

2/10

If it were a film the suggested MPAA Ratingwould be PG-13 for Intense Sci/Fi Violence and Disturbing Content including Frightening Images, Sexuality, Drug Content and some Language

At some points we see couples, presumably after sex, in bed together. At at least four points, couples sensually grasp and straddle each other in bed.

There is some kissing as well.

Violence & Gore

Most episodes involve dead bodies. Usually disturbing.

Profanity

Swearing does not occur often.

D*mn B*tch S.O.B Hell

Alcohol/Drugs/Smoking

Walter does enjoy what he calls "self-medicating" himself but we never see him its only talked about. Many experiments involve drugs, especially LSD.

Some characters were experimented on as children with an extreme, experimental drug.

Frightening/Intense Scenes

6/10

While Fringe is not all that graphic there are some episodes that can be very frightening I'll give a list of episodes to look out for:

Pilot (Graphic and disturbing) The Same Old Story (Disturbing.) The Cure (Very graphic and also highly disturbing. Arguably the nastiest Fringe episode) In Which We Meet Mr. Jones (Frightening, Graphic, and Disturbing) The Equation (Disturbing, Mildly Frightening) Bound (Disturbing, Mildly Frightening) The Transformation (Graphic) Ability (Disturbing) Unleashed (Extremely Graphic, Mildly Frightening) Bad Dreams (Mildly graphic and disturbing.) Midnight (Graphic) The Road Not Taken (Very frightening and mildly graphic) Night of Desirable Objects (Frightening) Earthling (Frightening) Of Human Action (Very frightening and and also very disturbing) Snakehead (Very graphic and mildly frightening) Johari Window (Grapic, frightening and mildly disturbing) What Lies Below (Very frightening and disturbing) Olivia. In the Lab. With the Revolver. (Frightening) The Man From The Other Side (Frightening) Do Shapeshifters Dream of Eletric Sheep (Disturbing and mildly graphic) Amber 31422 (Mildly disturbing) The Abducted (Very disturbing) Entrada (Mildly graphic) Marionette (Mildly graphic) Reciprocity (Graphic) Immortality (Mildly graphic)
